Title

Certified Laboratory List

A laboratory/IITF that is interested in becoming HHS-certified and performing regulated drug testing under the NLCP is required to submit an application. For information concerning the NLCP application process please contact the National Laboratory Certification Program (NLCP) at (919) 541-7242 or email NLCP@rti.org.

Body

Find HHS-certified laboratories who may conduct drug testing for federal agencies and specific federally regulated industries.

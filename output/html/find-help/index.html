Find Help & Support

FindTreatment.gov

The confidential and anonymous resource for persons seeking treatment for mental and substance use disorders in the United States and its territories. Available in Spanish.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

24-hour, toll-free, confidential support for people in distress. Prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones.
Call or text 988.

Veterans Crisis Line

Reach caring, qualified responders with the Department of Veterans Affairs. Many of them are Veterans themselves.
Dial 988 then press 1.

FindSupport.gov

Find Support is an online guide that helps people navigate through common questions when they are at the start of their journey to better behavioral health. Available in Spanish.

SAMHSA's National Helpline

24/7 free and confidential treatment referral and information about mental and substance use disorders, prevention, and recovery.
Call 1-800-662-HELP.

Disaster Distress Helpline

24/7, 365-day-a-year crisis counseling and support to people experiencing emotional distress related to natural or human-caused disasters.

Disaster Preparedness, Response, Recovery, and Mitigation

Supporting the management of behavioral health effects from disasters.

Screen4Success

Screen4Success provides an easy way for parents and caregivers to identify areas where their children may benefit from additional support.

Drug-Free Workplace Helpline

Confidential help for employees and their families to resolve substance misuse and drug testing issues.

Buprenorphine Practitioner Locator

Find practitioners authorized to treat opioid dependency with buprenorphine by state.

Opioid Treatment Program Directory

Find treatment programs in your state that treat addiction and dependence on opioids.

Early Serious Mental Illness Treatment Locator

Find treatment programs in your state that treat recent onset of serious mental illnesses.
